Pruitt, Rays bullpen combine to top Keuchel, Astros 3-0

John Doe
August 3, 2017 Filed in Football

Apparently the good folks at MaxPreps now realize what Central Texans have known for the last decade.

Lake Travis is the best high school football program in the state over the past 10 years, and MaxPreps has the formula to prove it.

MaxPreps put together a list of the 15 most dominant football programs in Texas since 2006. The website awarded 20 points for a state championship, which accounted for a whooping 120 points tallied by the Cavs. Teams also received 10 for a state championship game appearance Other points are based on where a team finished in the final MaxPreps Texas Football Computer Rankings.For example, if a team finished No. 1 overall, it would receive 25 points. If it finished 25th overall, the team would get one point. No points are awarded if the team did not finish in the Top 25.

Based on that formula, Lake Travis tallied 299 points to edge Allen, which had 292 points.

Lake Travis has some familiar company on the list. Westlake, the Cavs' District 25-6A rival, ranked 12th with 104 points.

Two state championships and one state runner-up finish couldn't help Cedar Park crack the list.
